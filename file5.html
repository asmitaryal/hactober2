The meaning of 7 colors of the rainbow

Hello World!

1.The red color on the rainbow is adapted as the color of young blood with a surge of enthusiasm. Red rainbow is also interpreted as a symbol of pure love.

2.The orange color in the rainbow shows welfare in health. Balance between health and strength. The orange color is also owned by healthy fruits and vegetables, such as carrots and pumpkin.

3.Yellow means the color of joy, excitement and eternal pleasure and a warm atmosphere.

4.Tilapia means a very basic foundation with all the simplicity that appears to it. colors that according to Surrender, surrender, and accept whatever happens.

5.Warna ungu banyak disimbolkan sebagai keagungan seorang raja yang bijaksana. Dengan tingkat kemewahan yang tinggi, aristokrat. Yang penuh akan aksesoris dan perlengkapan.
